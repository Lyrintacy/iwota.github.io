The Designer

"I make the things that crawl in the beautiful dark"

I'm a game designer and systems architect obsessed with the space where procedural generation meets hand-crafted emotion. I believe the best games feel alive — like small ecosystems that breathe, mutate, and surprise even their creators.

My work draws from the grotesque beauty of artists like Edmund McMillen, the systemic depth of Dwarf Fortress, and the raw energy of Gogol Bordello. Start wearing purple.

Every system I design asks one question: "What happens if we let it grow?"

Design Mantras

  • Systems over scripts
  • Emergent stories beat written ones
  • Ugly-beautiful is a valid aesthetic
  • If the AI surprises you, you've won
  • Complexity hidden behind simplicity
