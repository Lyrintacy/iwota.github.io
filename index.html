The Designer

"I make the things that crawl in the beautiful dark"

I'm a game designer obsessed with the space where procedural generation meets hand-crafted emotion. The best games feel alive — ecosystems that breathe, mutate, and surprise even their creators.

My work draws from Edmund McMillen's grotesque beauty, Dwarf Fortress's systemic depth, and Gogol Bordello's raw energy.

Every system asks one question: "What happens if we let it grow?"

Design Mantras

  • Systems over scripts
  • Emergent stories beat written ones
  • Ugly-beautiful is valid
  • If the AI surprises you, you've won
  • Complexity hidden behind simplicity
